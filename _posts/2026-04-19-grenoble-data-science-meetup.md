I am part of the organizing team for the Grenoble Data Science meetup.
We typically meet once a month in Grenoble for a technical talk (in English) on data science, machine learning, or AI, followed by a social and technical discussion over food and drinks.

If you’d like to attend a talk, join the community, or learn more, check out [our site](https://sites.google.com/view/grenobledatascience/) or join our [LinkedIn group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/15454046/). **If you'd like to present**,  contact me or one of the other organizers.
