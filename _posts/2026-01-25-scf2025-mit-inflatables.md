Today I'm just proudly sharing my son's [paper](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3745778.3766669) presented at [SCF2025](https://scf.acm.org/2025/) at MIT 😊

[Ofir Mirkin](https://ofirmirkin.github.io/) and his co-authors introduce a new type of flat-fabricated inflatable structures that we use to approximate the shapes of target developable surfaces. In other words, they program flat sheets so that when they’re inflated, they naturally morph into a desired 3D shape, like the deckchair in the image above.

This work was done in collaboration with Nathan Vani, Etienne Reyssat, José Bico, and Mélina Skouras.
